Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our remote internship

How long is the internship?

➕

The internship lasts 4–6 weeks depending on performance.

Is the internship paid?

➕

This is an unpaid internship designed for learning and growth.

Will I get a certificate?

➕

Yes, you’ll receive a verified certificate after successful completion.

Is it a remote internship?

➕

Yes, all tasks are completed remotely with flexible working hours.

What skills are required?

➕

Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript is recommended.

How do I submit my tasks?

➕

You can submit tasks via GitHub, zipped folders, or live demo links.
